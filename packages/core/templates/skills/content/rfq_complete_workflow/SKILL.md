# RFQ Complete Workflow

## Overview

Complete RFQ lifecycle management for MeatConnect B2B marketplace, from validation through award.

## Process

1. **Validation**: Validate RFQ completeness and pricing structure
2. **Supplier Matching**: Match suppliers based on capabilities and requirements
3. **Bid Management**: Collect and manage supplier bids
4. **Award Process**: Evaluate bids and award contract

## Combines Skills

- rfq_validation
- supplier_matching
- bid_management
- award_process

## When to Use

- Processing new RFQs from buyers
- Managing supplier bidding process
- Awarding contracts to suppliers

**Suitable for**: MeatConnect RFQ operations
