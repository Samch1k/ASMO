# RFQ Complete Workflow - Examples

## Example: Beef Supply RFQ

**RFQ Details**:
- Product: 500kg premium beef
- Delivery: Moscow, within 7 days
- Budget: ₽250,000

**Workflow**:

1. **Validation** (15min):
   - Check all required fields present
   - Validate pricing structure (per kg pricing)
   - RFQ approved ✅

2. **Supplier Matching** (30min):
   - 15 suppliers match criteria (beef, Moscow region, capacity > 500kg)
   - Filter by ratings > 4.0: 8 suppliers
   - Notify 8 matched suppliers

3. **Bid Management** (2-3 days):
   - Receive 6 bids
   - Bid range: ₽420-520 per kg
   - Track bid submission timeline

4. **Award Process** (1 hour):
   - Evaluate bids (price, rating, delivery time)
   - Top bidder: ₽450/kg, rating 4.8, delivery 5 days
   - Award contract to supplier_id=123
   - Notify all bidders

**Total**: ~3 days (mostly waiting for bids)
